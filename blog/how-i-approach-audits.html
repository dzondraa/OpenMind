How I Approach Technical Audits

2025-08-15

Audits should produce clear, actionable outcomes—not binders. I use a lightweight framework that scores risks, quantifies impact, and proposes a prioritized roadmap.

  • Clarify goals and constraints with product and engineering
  • Assess architecture, code health, delivery, and operations
  • Highlight top risks and opportunities with evidence
  • Recommend 30/60/90 day actions with expected impact
